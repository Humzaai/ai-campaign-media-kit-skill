## Sample: honest stop rule (process page)

> **If week-one signals miss the floor we agreed on, we tell you — and recommend stopping.**
> A campaign that isn't landing doesn't get quietly extended. You get the data, the diagnosis, and a call: fix the angle, or stop spending.

This is the no-fake-certainty stance rendered as copy. It reduces risk, which is a conversion job.
