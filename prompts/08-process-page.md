# Prompt 08 — Process Page

---

Build `/process` — the page that turns "trust me" into "here is the operating system."

Sections, in order:

1. **Hero** — the promise: campaigns run on a system, not vibes
2. **Framework** — the named framework and its stages
3. **Why most campaigns fail** — honest diagnosis buyers recognize
4. **Operating system** — how a campaign actually runs week to week
5. **Format decision system** — how formats get chosen per goal
6. **Signal reading** — which signals get watched, and what they trigger
7. **Scale/stop rules** — explicit rules for doubling down and for stopping. Honest stop rules are a trust feature — keep them blunt.
8. **What clients get** — reporting cadence, access, artifacts
9. **Final CTA** — Work With Us / email

Rules:

- Name and describe the real process from intake. If the founder has no named framework, help them name their actual process — do not invent stages they don't follow.
- No guarantee language anywhere.
- One H1, mobile-first, clear CTA.

Output: the finished page and a one-paragraph summary of the framework as implemented.
