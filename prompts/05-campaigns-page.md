# Prompt 05 — Campaigns Page

---

Build `/campaigns` — the offer page. This is where a buyer decides the mechanism is real.

Sections, in order:

1. **Hero** — what a campaign is here, and why it beats a one-off sponsorship
2. **Problem** — why most sponsored posts fail the buyer
3. **Sponsorship vs campaign comparison** — honest side-by-side
4. **Campaign engine** — how the system works end to end
5. **Campaign types** — from `data/campaigns.ts`: use-when, what's included, expected outcome shape (no invented numbers)
6. **Campaign assets** — what gets produced (formats, deliverables)
7. **Audience proof** — snapshot from `data/audience.ts`, link to `/audience`
8. **Case-study proof** — strongest relevant results, link to `/case-studies`
9. **Signal system** — what gets measured and how decisions are made
10. **Risk reduction** — honest stop rules, no guarantees
11. **Campaign fit checker** — "this is for you if / not for you if"
12. **What happens next** — the exact steps after contact
13. **FAQ** — buyer objections about pricing, timelines, control, measurement
14. **Final CTA** — Work With Us / email with prefilled subject

Rules: proof-led claims only, no fake urgency, one H1, every claim traceable to intake data or marked `PENDING`.

Output: the finished page and a note on which objections the FAQ handles.
