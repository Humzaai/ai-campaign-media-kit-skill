# Prompt 06 — Work With Us Page

---

Build `/work-with-us` — the conversion page. Everything here reduces friction for a buyer who is already interested.

Sections, in order:

1. **Hero** — direct: how to start, what to expect
2. **What the client gets** — deliverables and access, concretely
3. **Who this is for** — qualify honestly; disqualify bad fits
4. **Campaign options** — from `data/campaigns.ts`, brief, linking back to `/campaigns`
5. **What happens after contact** — step-by-step with rough timing
6. **What to include in your email** — a short checklist that makes the first email easy to send
7. **Risk reduction** — stop rules, no lock-in claims that aren't real
8. **Audience proof** — brief snapshot, link to `/audience`
9. **Process proof** — brief, link to `/process`
10. **Reporting preview** — what reporting looks like (real format only; placeholder if pending)
11. **FAQ** — logistics: response time, lead time, payment process (only what's true)
12. **Email CTA** — prominent mailto with prefilled subject and the checklist recap

Rules: this page has one job — get a qualified email sent. Remove anything that doesn't serve it. No forms unless intake specified one; email-first by default.

Output: the finished page and the exact prefilled email subject/body used.
