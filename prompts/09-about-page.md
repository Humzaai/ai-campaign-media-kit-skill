# Prompt 09 — About Page

---

Build `/about` — the founder story page. Buyers fund people, not pages.

Sections, in order:

1. **Founder story hero** — who the founder is, in one strong statement
2. **Origin** — where this started, concretely
3. **Turning point** — the moment the approach changed or proved itself
4. **Why clients trust the founder** — proof-backed, not adjectives
5. **Numbers** — the founder's verified numbers from the data layer
6. **Philosophy** — how they think about campaigns, audience, and honesty (align with `SOUL.md` themes: proof, restraint, no fake certainty)
7. **Final CTA** — Work With Us / email

Rules:

- Use the real founder story from intake. Do not dramatize or invent biography.
- Short paragraphs; the story serves credibility, not autobiography.
- Founder photo goes through the media quality gate; pending placeholder if not provided.
- One H1, clear CTA.

Output: the finished page.
