# Prompt 04 — Homepage

---

Build the homepage (`/`) following `knowledge/copywriting-rules.md` and `knowledge/conversion-principles.md`.

Sections, in order:

1. **Hero** — positioning statement, who it's for, primary CTA + one secondary link to `/campaigns`
2. **Trust metrics** — 3–4 strongest verified numbers from `data/audience.ts` / proof metrics
3. **What we do** — the mechanism in one short section
4. **Campaigns preview** — campaign types from `data/campaigns.ts`, linking to `/campaigns`
5. **Case-study preview** — 2–3 strongest results, linking to `/case-studies`
6. **Audience preview** — snapshot linking to `/audience`
7. **Process preview** — the framework in brief, linking to `/process`
8. **Founder preview** — short credibility block linking to `/about`
9. **FAQ** — 4–6 real buyer objections
10. **Final CTA** — Work With Us / email

Rules:

- Every section must increase desire, increase trust, reduce risk, explain the mechanism, or make the next step easier — otherwise cut it.
- Short paragraphs, outcome-first headlines, no hype.
- `PENDING` proof renders as a polished placeholder, never a fake number.
- One H1. Mobile-first — verify at 320px.

Output: the finished homepage, plus one line per section stating which of the five jobs it does.
